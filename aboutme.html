About Me

My photo of the Northern Lights taken just outside Ithaca, October 11th, 2024.

I am originally from Atlanta, Georgia, where I attended Woodward Academy for high school. I majored in Physics and minored in Astronomy at Cornell University.

Public outreach in astronomy is very important to me, and I am currently part of the team that runs the Astronomy on Tap event in Ann Arbor. Additionally, while at Cornell I was a member of the Cornell Astronomical Society where we hosted observing open houses and tours of the historical Fuertes Observatory. I am also passionate about uplifing early-career researchers, and served as a co-editor-in-chief of the Cornell Undergraduate Research Journal while at Cornell.

Outside of academics and research, I love to spend time outdoors, whether hiking, paddleboarding, or just taking in the scenery. I am also a big fan of D&D and other pen-and-paper RPGs, and am learning how to print and paint my own miniatures for those games.
